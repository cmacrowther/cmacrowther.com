SquawkBox

SquawkBox is a virtual jukebox. Squawkbox allows users to attach their Spotify accounts and host virtual jukebox sessions. Hosts can invite other users to join their sessions and begin queueing up tracks to play. Once connected to an audio source, the hosts phone effectively becomes a jukebox!

Tags: Ionic, Node.js, Angular 2, HTML, CSS, Mobile App
